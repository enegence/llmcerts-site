## Design Context

### Users
Working professionals upskilling for AI certifications, primarily developers — in that order. They're goal-oriented, time-conscious, and skeptical of fluff. They arrive knowing what certifications exist and want to evaluate whether LLMCerts is worth their time.

### Brand Personality
Rigorous, credible, modern.

### Aesthetic Direction
The existing design direction should be preserved — dark navy hero with emerald green accent, transitioning to a light content body. Not a redesign; polish only. Anti-reference: avoid anything that looks like a generic edtech SaaS (pastel gradients, friendly rounded blobs, "learning journey" language).

### Design Principles
1. Credibility over enthusiasm — every element should feel earned, not marketed
2. Hierarchy through contrast, not decoration
3. Restraint in color use — the green accent earns its impact by being rare
4. Typography should feel editorial, not corporate-template
5. No design element that doesn't serve a function
